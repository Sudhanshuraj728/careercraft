About CareerCraft

CareerCraft revolutionizes your job search with AI-powered resume analysis and intelligent career insights. Our platform uses advanced artificial intelligence to evaluate your resume, provides company-specific optimization tips, and connects you with professionals through seamless LinkedIn integration. Get personalized feedback, discover what top companies are looking for, and network with employees at your dream companies—all in one powerful platform.

Help & FAQ

How do I get started?

Create an account, upload your resume in the Upload section, and let our AI analyze it. You'll get instant feedback and improvement suggestions.

How do I connect my LinkedIn account?

After signing in, go to your dashboard and click the "Link LinkedIn" button. You'll be redirected to LinkedIn where you need to click "Allow" to authorize the connection. You'll be automatically brought back to CareerCraft.

What resume formats are supported?

We support PDF, DOC, and DOCX formats. For best results, use a PDF with clear text (not scanned images).

How does the AI analysis work?

Our AI analyzes your resume content, format, keywords, and structure. It provides feedback on improvements, missing skills, and optimization for ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems).

Can I get company-specific advice?

Yes! Add target companies to your dashboard and get tailored suggestions based on their job requirements and culture.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Your resume and personal data are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties.

What's included in the premium subscription?

Premium users get unlimited resume analyses, LinkedIn integration, company contact search, priority support, and advanced AI insights.
